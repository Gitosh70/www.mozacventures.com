Who are we?

We are a world’s leading inspection, Testing ,Training & Consultancy Company. We are recognized as the global benchmark for quality and integrity. With more than 60 employees, we operate a network of more than 25 offices around the world.

MOZAC VENTURES offers services and solutions that are long-lasting, trustworthy, and of high quality in areas related to Quality, Safety, Health, and Environment.
We actively follow the S.H.E (Safety, Health, and Environment) process and highlight the importance of taking proactive approaches and adopting the right behavior to foster a culture that values S.H.E within organizations. Being proactive is crucial for building a positive corporate culture and helps organizations avoid losses in terms of human resources, assets, and business.
