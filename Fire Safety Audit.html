Fire Safety Audit

We examine your company premises to ascertain how they are managed in terms of fire safety Current legislative requirements place the
emphasis on the employer / landlord to effectively identify, control and mitigate the risk of fire within any premises in which they are deemed
to be the responsible person. It is therefore essential from a moral, legal and financial perspective that fire safety management arrangements
are effectively implemented, reviewed and continually improved.

Why go for a FSA?

Fire Safety Audits are usually pre-arranged and can be conducted for a number of reasons:

  • A complaint
  • A fire
  • A follow-up audit
  • On request, i.e., if an owner or occupier requests an audit
  • In conjunction with other authorities, i.e., if you've submitted a premises license application
